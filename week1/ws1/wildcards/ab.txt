Albania, officially known as the Republic of Albania, is
 a country in Southeastern Europe. It is bordered by 
Montenegro to the northwest, Kosovo to the northeast, 
the Republic of Macedonia to the east, and Greece to the 
south and southeast. It has a coast on the Adriatic Sea 
to the west and on the Ionian Sea to the southwest. It is 
less than 72 km (45 mi) from Italy, across the Strait of 
Otranto which links the Adriatic Sea to the Ionian Sea.
